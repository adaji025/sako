Frequently Asked Questions

You are at the right place. Here we provide more information on things you might need more explanation for.

What is Sako

Sako is a delivery service company that offers services such as online food orders, pickups, deliveries and errands.

What can I do with Sako?

Sako enables you to order your favourite food from any restaurant and delivers it to your doorstep. Sako also carries out errands.

Do you deliver outside Minna?

Currently we do not but in the future we do hope to expand.

How fast will it take my order to arrive?

Our aim is to provide the best delivery services as quick as possible. Consequently,the timing of your order is determined by taking account the number of orders and the circumstances being faced by partner service provider at that time.

How much does it cost for pickup and delivery

It depends on your location of the pickup and delivery, but you can always try our plans to get good deals and discounts.

Do you deliver bulky and heavy items?

Yes we do, they are called special orders, you can call or text us to place your order or errands.

Can I use your address for my orders coming into Minna?

Yes you can, you can come to our store to pick it up or we can deliver it to you, all you have to do is send a messages or call to identify that it belongs to you.

What are your opening and closing times?

7am - 10pm daily

Do you have a mobile app?

Yes we do but it is still being processed, kindly write down your email address in the input ox on our website, to get notified when the app is ready for use.

How does pricing works

Our pricing works based on the location and type of errand. you can subscribe to any of our plans to get good deals and discounts

How do I pay for my orders?

Can I pay on delivery?

Unfortunately no you can't, but we do hope that in the future we will be able to include an option to pay on delivery.

Can I schedule a delivery

Yes you can.

Can I change my order?

Order can only be changed before confirmation. however, orders done after confirmation orders can't be changed.

Who pays for a lost or damaged package?

Our company will take full responsibility for any damaged package.
